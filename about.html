About Us

Chevy Chase Dog Walks is a local dog walking service run by people who are passionate about dogs and enjoy spending time with them.
Our goal is to make sure every dog enjoys their walk and gets the care, attention, and exercise they deserve.
We are available Monday,Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday with varying hours. We only provide services to residents in Chevy Chase DC.
We walk dogs for one hour priced at 30 dollars .
