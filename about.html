About Us

Chevy Chase Dog Walks is a local dog walking service run by teenagers who are passionate about dogs and enjoy spending time with them.
Our goal is to make sure every dog enjoys their walk and gets the care, attention, and exercise they deserve.
We are available Monday,Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday with varying hours and only provide services to residents in Chevy Chase DC.
We offer 1 hour dog walks for 30$ or 30 minute dog walks for 20$
The business is currently undergoing an expansion in order to offer services daily.
We also have plans to add other services such as dog sitting.
We always strive to provide owners with low prices and excellent services.
