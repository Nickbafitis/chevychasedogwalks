About Us

Chevy Chase Dog Walks is a local dog walking service run by teenagers who are passionate about dogs and enjoy spending time with them.
Our goal is to make sure every dog enjoys their walk and gets the care, attention, and exercise they deserve.
We are available all week with varying hours and only provide services to residents in Chevy Chase DC (for now).
We offer 1 hour dog walks for 30$ 30 minute dog walks for 20$, or 15 minutes dog walks for 10$.
We always strive to provide owners with low prices and excellent services.

About the founder

Nikolaos Bafitis founded the Chevy Chase dog walks company in 2026 with the hope of pursuing what he loves,pets.
He is 15 years old and is a student at Walt Whitman Highschool. Since he already loved pets, he decided to start a business that revolved around them.
Using his coding skills he made a website for the company in order to facilitate the booking process.
Now he has advanced the business to the point where he is hiring other highschool students and providing them with a job they can enjoy.
